How do I setup TurboTax software?

  1. Download the Software:
    • Visit installturbotax.com.
    • Navigate to the download page and choose the version of TurboTax you need.
    • Click the download link to save the installation file to your computer.
  2. Run the Installer:
    • Locate the downloaded file on your computer (usually in the "Downloads" folder).
    • Double-click the file to start the installation process.
  3. Begin Installation:
    • A setup window will appear. Follow the on-screen prompts to begin the installation.
    • You may need to agree to the license agreement before proceeding.
  4. Enter Your License Code:
    • During the installation process, you'll be prompted to enter your license code. This code is typically found in your purchase confirmation email or on the product packaging.
    • Enter the code and click "Next" to continue.
  5. Select Installation Location:
    • Choose the destination folder where you want TurboTax to be installed. The default location is usually fine for most users.
    • Click "Install" to begin the installation.
  6. Complete Installation:
    • Wait for the installation to complete. This may take a few minutes.
    • Once the installation is finished, click "Finish" to close the setup window.
  7. Launch TurboTax:
    • Open TurboTax from your desktop or start menu.
    • If you already have an account, sign in with your email and password. If you don't have an account, you'll need to create one by following the on-screen prompts.
  8. Get Started:
    • After signing in, you'll be guided through the initial setup process where you can enter your personal information, income details, and other tax-related data.
    • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your tax return.

How do I enter my W-2 information?

Quick Steps to Enter Your W-2 Information in TurboTax

  1. Open TurboTax and log in to your account.
  2. Select "Start a New Tax Return" or continue an existing one.
  3. Navigate to "Federal Taxes" > "Income & Wages" > "Wages and Salaries (W-2)."
  4. Import or Enter Manually:
    • Import: Enter your employer’s EIN to retrieve your W-2.
    • Manual Entry: Input details from your W-2 form, including wages, taxes withheld, and employer information.
  5. Double-check all entries for accuracy.
  6. Click "Done" or "Continue" to move to the next section.
